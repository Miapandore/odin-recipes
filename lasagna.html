Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is probably the most wellknown italian pasta classic dish.

Find our most authentic recipe here. Nothing but the best basic ingredient, without noise or distraction.

ingredient

  • 340 ml tomato sauce
  • lasagna pasta
  • mozzarella cheese
  • basil
  • parsley, for presentation

Steps

  1. turn over on at 350F
  2. Boil pasta until half cooked
  3. In lasagna dish, layer pasta, tomato sauce and cheese, finishig with cheese
  4. Cover with foil and place in over for 45 mins to 1hr.
  5. Cut in parts and serve with parsley garnish.
